Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the warranty period for appliances?

Ans.Most of our appliances come with a standard manufacturer’s warranty of 1 year. Some products may offer extended warranties, which will be detailed in the product description.

2. Can I return an appliance if it’s not working?

Ans.Yes, you can return any faulty appliance within 30 days of purchase. Please refer to our returns policy for detailed instructions.

3. Do you offer installation services for large appliances?

Ans.Absolutely! We offer professional installation services for large appliances such as refrigerators, washing machines, and dishwashers. You can select this option at checkout.

4. What are the energy efficiency ratings of your appliances?

Ans.Our appliances range from A+++ to D in energy efficiency ratings. Each product listing includes detailed information about its energy consumption.

5. Can I purchase appliances on finance?

Ans.Yes, we offer flexible financing options. You can choose to pay in installments with 0% interest for up to 12 months.
